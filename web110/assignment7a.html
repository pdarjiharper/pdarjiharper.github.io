I wanted to then start organizing my thoughts and started thinking of how I would structure my presentation. I thought of breaking it down into sections by starting with the history/origin, purpose of it, benefits/disadvantages, popular social media platforms, privacy, and security. History and origins will go into how social media came to be. The purpose of social media is going to talk about why social media has become so important along benefits and disadvantages. I will cover the popular social media apps and what they are used for. Finally, I will go into Privacy and Security which will be important to keep your information private I am going to show you how you can make your information private.
